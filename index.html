About Hargow

Hargow helps you keep track of your pantry, reduce food waste, and find recipes using ingredients you already have. Powered by a friendly AI dumpling, Hargow reminds you when things are about to expire — so your meals stay fresh and fun.

The app is designed for busy home cooks who want to make smart, sustainable choices without extra effort.
